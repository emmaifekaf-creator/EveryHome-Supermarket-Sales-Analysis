## Project Background

everyHome Supermarket is experiencing operational challenges in their three branches: paying a lot of overtime, customers complaining about wait times, erratic satisfaction ratings. There are fears that their ads are tergetting wrong demographics and their member loyalty program may noit be yeilding results.
The project analyzes available supermarket data and produces an interactive Excel dashboard with actionable recommendations.


## Project objective

- Identify root causes of overtime payments, customers wait times, erractic satisfaction ratings.
- Analyze success and failure of Member Loyalty Program, marketing ads based on demographic
- Build an interactive Excel dashboardcto inform board level decisions.
- Recommend prioritized actions to enhance sales performance optimization.


## Datasets

** Supermarket_sales_2025.csv ** - invoice ID, City, Customer Type, Gender, Payment, Product Line, Quantity, Unit Price, Total, COGS, Date, Time, Rating


## Dashboard Features

- Key KPIs: Total transaction, Sum of total amount spent by customers.
- Visuals: Rush hour/Day Chart, Member Vs Normal Chart, Product Line chart, Rating chart  based on Sum of Total amount spent, number of transactrions, city, Day,Time
- Interactivity: Slicers/filters to City,Product Line, Customer Type, Gender, Day, Rating(group).



## Key Findings

- Members(Customer Type) have more foot traffic and total amount spent at Austun and Chicago branch, but reverse is the casde at New York branch.
- Austin and New York branch usually have their peak rush hour at 17hrs while Chicago is at 20hrs.
- Average Amount spent per customer in New York and Chicago is higher for Normal(Customer Type). Only at Austin is average spent per customer higher for members.
- More Male purchased Electronic accessories batb Chicago and New York whileb Health & Beauty products where evenly bought from Male and Female.
- Higher satisfaction rate for customers that paid highest amount.
- Low ratings appear most on Saturdays followed by Thursdays.
- The value of Ads budget is not available so as to determine ROAS or ROI.
- Number of transactions evenly distributed among all product line.



## Recommendations

- Member Loyalty Program for Nwe York and Chicago branch should be scrapped. The package can be enhanvced for Austin branch and observed for another year.
- Markatin team should discontinue with the digital ads for Electronics, Health & Beauty products.
- Customers should only be encouraged to rate, but also to give feedback so as to easily notice cause of disatisfaction and low rating.
- Periodic training for staff on best customer relationship practices.
- Modern sales software (CRM) should be used to run operations in those three branches.
